Schlumberger needed to re-fresh its tradeshow look & feel. Outdated false brushed-metal textures and chrome fixtures had adorned their booths for years, I was asked to come up with some new solutions. I created these lo-fi 3D models to visualize and discuss my ideas with the larger team. Ultimately, no action was taken due to budget constraints, etc.
