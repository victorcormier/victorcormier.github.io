Keane Look&Feel Redesign

I was approached by Keane Group, an oil and gas operator headquartered here in Houston, to help them revise their look & feel.

After some discussion on the type-heavy direction I wanted to head, I came up with a new overall look, applied it to a number of their collateral types, and built out these slides for Keane's marketing team to pitch to the higher-ups.

Well received, the Keane team and I are still working together within this framework, with dozens of individual projects completed.
