Sample screens from the iOS version of The Science of Shale

The Science of Shale was a publication created to broadcast Schlumberger's then-current understanding of shale gas & liquids technology. It was created and published as a perfect-bound booklet and an interactive iPad app. This was a longer project for what was essentially a single piece, months of editorial and content work for the team alongside the design/production work for me. It was a learning experience as the company's first substantial purpose-built tablet publication.
