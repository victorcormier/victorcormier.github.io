One of my early solo projects for ADV Films was handling the packing design for their DVD box-set re-release of the Ruroni Kenshin animated television series.

Working from Japanese-provided lineart, the published print artwork was re-traced and re-styled to incorporate original concept paintings as textures throughout the covers.
