About

I live and work in Houston, TX along with my wife and two dogs. I've worked for the last 12 years as a graphic designer and art director, previously as an employee of ADV Films and Schlumberger, and for the past couple years freelance.

The majority of my work has been print-focused graphic and layout design; with web design, UI, and illustration projects whenever the opportunity has presented.
